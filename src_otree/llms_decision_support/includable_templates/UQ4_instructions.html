Part 1: Decision Selection

The fixed cost is independent of the actual coffee flow. Even if the coffee flow to or from a supplier or roastery is zero, you have to pay the fixed cost if you previously decided to activate them.

Shipping between suppliers, roasteries, and customers is optimized automatically, given your supplier and roastery activation decisions. Shipping cost per unit vary depending on the route.

Suppliers can be set inactive or active. Clicking on a supplier will activate it, clicking again will deactivate it. Roasteries can be set inactive, active with 'Low' capacity or active with 'High' capacity. Clicking on a roastery allows you to toggle between all three states.
