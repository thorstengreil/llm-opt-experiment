Part 1: Decision Support

As decision support, you will be provided with a possible activation setting for the network which maximizes the average profit over many disruption scenarios, considering the disruption probabilities. However, this activation setting may carry more risk due to its exposure to potential disruptions, than other activation settings. Consider this example: if you receive $50 with a 50% chance and $152 with the remaining 50%, this may be considered riskier than receiving $99 at 50% and $101 at 50%. On average both give you similar profits, $101 and $100, respectively. While the first profit is slightly higher, it may also be described as riskier.
