Compensation details

Your compensation consists of three parts.

(A)

You will receive a base compensation of {{ CURRENCY }} {{ BASE_PAYOFF }}.

(B)

If you answer all understanding questions on a page correctly on the first try, you will get an additional {{ CURRENCY }} {{ UQ_BONUS_PER_PAGE }} (per page). There are {{ NR_OF_UQ_PAGES }} pages to ensure your understanding before the experiment begins, i.e. you can earn a maximum bonus of {{ CURRENCY }} {{ UQ_BONUS_PER_PAGE }} × {{ NR_OF_UQ_PAGES }} = {{ CURRENCY }} {{ MAX_UQ_BONUS }} for this part.

If you fail to answer the questions on a page correctly in {{ MAX_UQ_TRIES }} tries, you will not be allowed to continue the experiment. In this case, your payoff will be the base compensation plus your accumulated bonus, if any.

(C)

You will additionally be paid based on your decisions and an element of chance. You can receive a maximum additional payoff of {{ CURRENCY }} {{ MAX_DECISIONS_PAYOFF }} (i.e. a total maximum payoff of {{ CURRENCY }} {{ TOTAL_MAX_PAYOFF }} incl. base payoff and full understanding bonus).
